Our Team

POLYGON FOUNDER

DILON HARPER
1999 till date​

Dilon Harper founded Polygon Plc in the year 1999. His vision for Polygon was to be one of the largest Construction Companies in Africa. He built Polygon, together with a strong management team, to stand shoulder to shoulder with the large construction companies in Africa. The current management team will continue to be driven by the founding values, passion and dedication that Dilon has instilled in the Company.

DIRECTORS

YIVONE GREY
Chief Executive Officer

Yvone joined Polygon Plc in 2007, taking on various management and strategic roles including managing the Special Projects division and Chairing the Board of Directors. In 2021 she was appointed as Chief Executive Officer of Trencon Construction. Prior to Trencon, she worked in various roles in Corporate Finance and Strategy. Her experience in the corporate world together with significant experience in the industry gained over the last 14 years, she will endeavour to keep Trencon’s vision alive. She graduated Cum Laude with a BComm Honours degree in Finance in 2000 from Wits University.

TONY MARCEL
Chief Operations Officer​

Tony joined Polygon Plc in 2013. He has been active in the civil and building industry since 1982 and holds a HND Civil Engineering from Wits. His career started as a civil engineering student with a major construction company, where he was eventually promoted to the position of Operational Director. During his career, he has been responsible for many landmark and prestigious projects. He has 39 years’ experience in the construction and building industries.

GARTH FORBES
Chief Operations Officer​

Garth joined Trencon in 2002 and holds a HND Building Surveying Diploma from Wits. He is a founding member of the company and has over 18 years experience in both the building and civils industry. He is responsible for all commercial aspects of our projects.
